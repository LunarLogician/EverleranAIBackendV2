# StudentApp - AI-Powered Learning Platform

StudentApp is an intelligent educational platform that transforms study materials into interactive learning experiences. Upload your documents and get instant access to AI-powered study tools.

## 🎯 What It Does

### **Document Processing**
- **Upload & Process**: Support for PDF, DOCX, DOC, and PPTX files
- **Smart Extraction**: Automatically extracts text content from various document formats
- **Cloud Storage**: Secure cloud storage for all your study materials

### **AI Study Assistant**
- **Q&A Chat**: Ask questions about your documents and get instant, accurate answers
- **Document Understanding**: AI comprehends your study materials like a human tutor
- **Interactive Learning**: Chat interface makes studying engaging and efficient

### **Study Tools Generator**
- **Flashcards**: Automatically generate flashcards from your documents for quick review
- **Quizzes**: Create custom quizzes to test your knowledge on any topic
- **Multiple Choice Questions**: Generate MCQs for exam preparation
- **Assignments**: Create and manage assignments based on your study materials

### **Smart Features**
- **Token-Based System**: Fair usage limits ensure quality service for everyone
- **Subscription Plans**: Free tier with premium features available
- **Usage Tracking**: Monitor your learning progress and token consumption
- **Google Authentication**: Secure and easy login with your Google account

## 🚀 How It Works

1. **Sign Up**: Login with your Google account - no password needed
2. **Upload Documents**: Add your PDF, Word, or PowerPoint files
3. **Start Learning**: Ask questions, generate flashcards, or create quizzes
4. **Track Progress**: Monitor your usage and learning journey
